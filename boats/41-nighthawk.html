41 Nighthawk

The innovative 41’ Nighthawk is designed to let you cruise the water in true Cigarette style. The Nighthawk offers the latest Cigarette Racing design, with its all new hard top. Loaded with state-of-the-art technology, the Nighthawk is as luxurious as it is thrilling to drive. Cigarette proves yet gain that high performance and comfort do not have to be mutually exclusive. The 41’ Nighthawk provides direct, unadulterated, performance combined with comfort that is like no other center console..

TWIN STEP HULL DESIGN

The Nighthawk draws on Cigarette’s advanced-engineered twin-step hull design to match unprecedented performance with unyielding comfort. A low, aggressive center of gravity and an all-carbon fiber hardtop design further improves handling, while intelligent use of composite materials and proprietary construction techniques improve vibration dampening and structural integrity for enhanced comfort in even the most compromised conditions.

ADVANCED HELM

The helm features three 17” Garmin flush mounted to an instrument panel CNC cut from a solid block of aluminum. The nano-coated instrument panel is resistant to heat, reflections, and fingerprints, and the perimeter of the panel is lit by RGB LED accent lighting to provide a distinct glow, creating the illusion that the dash is floating. The helm integrates cupholders and two gloveboxes. Passengers can feel secure with the stainless steel grab bars and be in total comfort with an ergonomic CNC machined footrest.

DIGITAL SWITCHING AND AUTOMATION

The Nighthawk features a state-of-the-art digital switching system allowing easy control of vessel functions. This system also features automatic sequential engine starting with the push of a single button.

LIGHTING WITHOUT LIMITS

The Nighthawk uses RGB ambient lighting that can be digitally controlled with multifunctional Garmin displays. Powered LED lighting with infinite color variations is featured throughout the deck including high powered down lights in the hardtop and red captains lighting. The technology extends seamlessly into an underwater RGB system to create the perfect ambiance for a night out on the water.

JL AUDIO X CIGARETTE RACING

For the finest marine sound systems, Cigarette relies on the leader of the industry, JL Audio Marine. Their products meet Cigarette’s pressing demand for audiophile quality systems that can withstand the rigors of the wind, sea and open cockpits. These sound systems are tested on each model to ensure they are appropriate for the boat size and seating configuration in order to provide the ultimate Cigarette Racing entertainment experience.

GARMIN X CIGARETTE RACING

When two iconic brands collaborate, the result is rather exciting. As the first manufacturer to be Garmin-Certified, Cigarette has proudly partnered with Garmin to feature state-of-the-art technology and design to give our owners the most ergonomic and intuitive resources to make a day out on the water easy, fun and safe. The optional Garmin Convenience package places a 9” multifunctional display at the aft conversational area and bow section, for additional control points of digital switching, media, and navigation. No need to get up, just tap the screen!

AN ATHLETIC HARDTOP

Its sculptured form creates an athletic disposition, alluding to the capabilities of this puristic center console. The four-pillar, all carbon fiber hardtop features a wide, double-curvature, frameless clear laminated safety glass windscreen offering exceptional protection from the elements. The hardtop includes integrated flood lights, boarding handrails, full-spectrum ambient lighting, electrically actuated sunshade, and optional windshield wipers with a cleaning system.

SUNSHADE

The Nighthawk’s hardtop offers an optional electrically actuated sunshade that, at the press of a button will cover the aft entertainment area. Ready to get underway? Simply press the “Shade In” button on your Garmin display and it will retract into the hardtop.

COMFORT AND LAYOUT

The all-new upholstery takes the hand-crafted Cigarette upholstery to a whole new level with multi-layered foams and highly detailed quilted patterns. The Cigarette “Cool Touch” Fabric upgrade allows for dark colors to be used in sunlight while the fabric remains cool to the touch.

CABIN OVERVIEW

The cabin offers over 6’ of headroom, luxurious cabinetry, a bed, storage, sink, and a vacu-flush head. Even a purpose-built day-boat should have a place to rest your head for a nap - and the Nighthawk offers just that.
