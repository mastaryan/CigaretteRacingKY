515 Cigarette

The 515 is the most innovative performance boat Cigarette has ever built. Utilizing the latest technologies and methods of design make it the flagship performance-oriented boat of the Cigarette model lineup. Confident, composed and exhilarating, the 515 demands to be driven. Each 515 is commissioned custom through Cigarette’s Design Center with immense personalization to reflect each owner’s unique identity.

Features

ENGINE DETAILS

- Twin Mercury Racing 1350/1100 - Twin Mercury Racing 1350/1550s With M8 Drives

DECK

The 515 deck applies carbon fiber, vacuum bagged sandwich construction to provide high rigidity at a lighter weight – over 1.7 x more flexural rigidity than typical construction methods. As the highest point on the boat, special consideration was given to keeping the deck light weight to optimize the performance dynamics of the boat.

SWIM PLATFORM AND HATCH LIFT INTEGRATION

Technologically advanced as it is beautiful, the carbon fiber engine hatch is strong, light weight and functional. Cigarette Racing stresses the smallest details which is evident with a hatch that exemplifies aesthetics while reducing weight for overall performance.

COCKPIT

The 515 is longer than its predecessor, the 50’ Marauder, by 1’6’’ and taller by 1’3’’ but the bulk of its increased size comes from the beam which is 1’6’’ wider. The result is a vessel over 30% larger in surface area with a cockpit that allows six comfortably seated passengers.

CONSOLE

The 515 consoles use a “glass cockpit” dash assembly with recessed 22” Garmin multifunction touch screen displays with GPS and Mercury integration for engine data. Control switches are illuminated push buttons; IP67 rated for extreme durability and the steering wheel is stainless steel featuring Cigarette’s logo embroidered on the center cap.

GARMIN X CIGARETTE RACING

When two iconic brands collaborate, the result is rather exciting! As the first manufacturer to be Garmin-Certified, Cigarette has proudly partnered with Garmin to feature state-of-the-art technology and design to give our owners the most ergonomic and intuitive resources to make a day out on the water fun and easy. The optional Garmin Convenience package places a 9” multifunctional display at the aft conversational area and bow section, for additional control points of digital switching, media, and navigation. No need to get up, just tap that screen!

CARBON FIBER BOLSTERS

The seat backs are meticulously constructed of pre-preg carbon fiber. The result is strong, beautiful, and light-weight.

CNC BOLSTER BASES

Cigarette’s attention to detail and dedication to perfection is prominently on display in its CNC milled aluminum pieces. Our custom-designed, precision milled pieces for the 515 are uniquely triple-anodized to produce a beautifully bespoke component of multiple colors.

IMPECCABLE CONSTRUCTION

Completely bespoke, each boat is fitted with a range of materials and in-house crafted embroidery.

ROOM FOR SIX

The expansive cockpit allows for six seats. There are integrated cockpit tubs under the rear seats, since these are drained, they may be used as coolers. These also have a gutter to help keep things dry if used for dry storage.

CARBON AIR INTAKE

Exposed weave pre-impregnated (“pre-preg”) carbon fiber air intakes contrast the painted body. Besides aesthetics, these pieces are also extremely light: the air intakes weigh only 4.4-lbs, despite being over 7ft long and 14-inches wide.

SPACIOUS CABIN

The 515 offers more head room height and a comfortable seating arrangement due to its deeper hull design and spacious 9’ 6” beam width. The added width also provides a more accommodating cabin door width and roomier steps accessing the forward deck of the boat. It also includes integrated, drain equipped storage boxes located on both port and starboard sides of the cabin that can be used as coolers. Additional storage is located behind the seats providing a great place to store towels and soft goods. The most notable aspect is the light-weight construction of the 515's cabin shell in carbon fiber, enabling the incorporation of all these amenities without a significant increase in weight.
