42' Auroris

The Auroris embraces over 50 years of performance boat building experience with the incorporation of Cigarette’s advanced-engineered twin-step hull design. This proprietary hull design provides extraordinary agility, industry-leading efficiency, and unparalleled comfort. A broader beam with an all carbon fiber hardtop design further improves handling, while intelligent use of composite materials and proprietary construction techniques improve vibration dampening and structural integrity for enhanced comfort in even the most compromised conditions. This luxurious center console is athletic, comfortable, powerful, and rich in features.

ADVANCED HELM

The Auroris’ advanced helm features three 17” Garmin multifunctional displays flush mounted to a state-of-the-art dash assembly milled from a solid block of aluminum that is anodized and hard coated for durability. It includes integrated cupholders, glovebox compartments, stainless steel grab bars and ergonomic CNC machined footrests that provide the convenience and comfort that is synonymous with Cigarette.

DIGITAL SWITCHING AND AUTOMATION

Our fully engineered and thoughtfully designed digital switching system allows you to control almost any function of the 42’ Auroris directly from the Garmin multifunctional displays. An automatic sequential engine system activates all engines with a simple press of a button.

OPTIONAL ADJUSTABLE HELM SEATING

The ergonomic helm seating is constructed with multi-density foam for comfort both at the dock and underway. Each of the three electric helm seats are independently adjustable, allowing for comfort at different heights and quickly convert from standard seating to standing bolsters for convenience and drive comfort in all conditions.

LIGHTING WITHOUT LIMITS

The Auroris uses full spectrum RGB zoned lighting that can be digitally controlled with multifunctional Garmin displays. A digitally controlled RGB ambient lighting system powers LED lighting with infinite color variations throughout the deck. The Auroris also features high powered LED down lights and red captain’s lights in the hardtop providing general and night task illumination. The technology extends seamlessly into an underwater RGB system to create the perfect ambiance for a night out on the water.

JL AUDIO X CIGARETTE RACING

For the finest marine sound systems, Cigarette relies on the leader in the industry, JL Audio Marine. Their products meet Cigarette’s pressing demand for audiophile quality systems that can withstand the rigors of the wind, sea and open cockpits. These sound systems are tested on each model to ensure they are appropriate for the boat size and seating configuration in order to provide the ultimate Cigarette Racing entertainment experience.

GARMIN X CIGARETTE RACING

As the first manufacturer to be Garmin-Certified, Cigarette Racing has proudly partnered with Garmin to feature state-of-the-art technology and design to provide our owners with the most ergonomic and intuitive resources to make a day on the water easy, fun, and safe. The optional Garmin Convenience package places a 9” multifunctional display at the aft conversational area and bow section for additional control points of digital switching, media, and navigation. No need to get up, just tap the screen!

CARBON FIBER HARDTOP

Our exclusive hardtop was skillfully engineered to provide a lower center of gravity to improve performance and comfort without compromising a beautiful design aesthetic. The four-pillar, all carbon fiber hardtop features a wide, double-curvature, and a frameless clear laminated safety glass windscreen offering exceptional protection from the elements. The hardtop includes integrated flood lights, boarding handrails, full-spectrum ambient lighting, electrically actuated sunshade, and optional windshield wipers with a cleaning system.

SUNSHADE

The Auroris hardtop offers an optional electrically actuated sunshade that, at the press of a button will cover the aft entertainment area. Ready to get underway? Simply press the “Shade In” button on your Garmin display and it will retract into the hardtop.

COMFORT AND LAYOUT

The Auroris features multi-layered foams coupled with detailed quilted patterns using Cigarette “Cool Touch” Fabric, a heat resistant fabric for premium comfort. Powered forward helm seats are independently adjustable and quickly convert from standard seating to standing bolsters for convenience and drive comfort in all conditions. Guests enjoy aft forward and rear facing seats that electronically extend to form an oversized sun lounge for the ultimate mix of comfort and style.

CABIN OVERVIEW

The Auroris features a spacious cabin with CNC cut cabinets and Corian countertops. Multi-color lighting allows the choice of normal daylight or ambient mood lighting. The cabin can be used to enjoy a meal amongst friends and family or for sleep by simply adjusting the table to bed height and placing the included cushion top.
