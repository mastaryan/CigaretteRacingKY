The 52’ Thunder is a new classic that was built for endless entertainment.

DECK LAYOUT

The 52’ Thunder is dedicated to on-board elegance and unequaled comfort. Guests can go for a swim through the dive door, refresh themselves with a drink from the aft summer kitchen or relax under the skylights beneath the hardtop. The 52’ Thunder is the ideal center console for luxurious day boating.

ADVANCED ENGINEERING

The Thunder encompasses over 55 years of performance boat building experience and advanced computational fluid dynamics by incorporating Cigarette Racing’s advanced-engineered twin-step hull design. Handcrafted in the USA, the proprietary hull design provides extraordinary performance, industry leading- efficiency and unparalleled comfort.

CARBON-FIBER HARDTOP

The four-pillar hardtop was skillfully engineered to provide a lower center of gravity in order to improve performance, yet not compromise comfort or aesthetics. This all carbon-fiber hardtop features double curvature, a frameless clear-laminated safety glass for wind protection, integrated flood lights, boarding handrails, ambient lighting, and a telescopic sun shade.

STATE-OF-THE-ART HELM

The Thunder’s advanced helm features dual, flush-mounted, 24” Garmin multifunction displays mounted on a nano-coated instrument panel that is heat, reflection, and fingerprint resistant. All guests will be able to enjoy the navigation details, and feel the all-powerful performance of the 52’ Thunder with multiple Garmin displays throughout the vessel. Additionally, the helm includes integrated cup and phone holders, stainless steel grab bars, and a side electric sliding door. All of these combine to skillfully provide the convenience and comfort that is synonymous with Cigarette Racing.

DIGITAL SWITCHING

This innovative design allows for simplistic control functions such as a push-button sequential engine start and three additional displays throughout The Thunder for even more accessibility.

SEVEN FORWARD FACING SEATS

The 52’ Thunder incorporates two rows of plush forward-facing seats that rest behind the helm. The four helm and co-driver seats are shifted forward to allow extra visibility and optimal control while the three second row seats are staggered to provide excellent forward visibility.

AFT LOUNGE ENTERTAINMENT AREA

Cigarette Racing elevates excellence to new heights! A luxury day boat, such as the 52’ Thunder, requires an ample entertainment area. Enjoy a serene afternoon by laying on the aft sun lounge under the telescopic sun shade for ultimate comfort. Or indulge in a delectable meal on the hydraulic table surrounded by your guests. It is the ultimate mix of comfort and style.

SUMMER KITCHEN

Enjoy a bite or refreshments from the 8’ wide multi-purpose summer kitchen. This luxurious convenience features a full-width lid with integrated lighting, a nano-coated anti-bacterial countertop with a plancha grill, a sink, and an abundance of storage.

ADVANCED MATERIALS AND PROCESSES

The Thunder uses advanced materials and processing methods to enhance performance and handling while dampening noise and vibration. By incorporating a broad beam width, a carbon fiber inner hull lining and an all-carbon fiber hardtop, The Thunder’s design enables unparalleled control on the water. The 52’ Thunder utilizes composite materials and proprietary construction techniques to improve structural integrity for enhanced comfort even in the most compromised conditions.

JL AUDIO X CIGARETTE RACING

For the finest marine sound systems, Cigarette relies on the leader of the industry, JL Audio Marine. Their products meet Cigarette’s pressing demand for audiophile quality that can withstand the rigors of the wind, sea and open cockpits. These sound systems are tested on each model to ensure they are appropriate for the boat size and seating configuration in order to provide the ultimate Cigarette Racing entertainment experience.

LIGHTING WITHOUT LIMITS

The Thunder uses RGB ambient lighting that can be digitally controlled with multifunctional Garmin displays. Powered LED lighting with infinite color variations is featured throughout the deck including high powered down lights in the hardtop and red captains lighting. The technology extends seamlessly into an underwater RGB system to create the perfect ambiance for a night out on the water.

CABIN

The Thunder’s cabin is the perfect retreat from the sun. Fully upholstered in premium bovine leather from world-famous Poltrona Frau, the cabin includes a full-size bed, enclosed head, refrigerator, hull side windows with electric privacy glass, a custom sofa, and air conditioning.

PAINT

The Cigarette World Famous Paint is essential and there are endless possibilities. Create a unique Thunder that reflects your own identity.
